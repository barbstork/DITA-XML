How to Sum Up Numbers

Here you will find instructions about using the sum up feature of the JPS Calculator app.

To successfully sum up numbers, you must know what summing up is.

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Press the plus (+) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Press the equal (=) button.

The result from the sum up function is displayed.

You want to sum up 2 and 3. The equation will look like: 2+3=5. You enter 3. Press plus. Enter 2. Press equals.

The result 5 appears on the screen.
